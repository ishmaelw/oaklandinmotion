NAVIGATING A CITY UNDER PRESSURE.

Oakland in Motion is a visual storytelling platform focused on helping residents understand how budget cuts, disinvestment, and public policy affect everyday life across Oakland communities.

Youth Programs

Learn how funding cuts impact after-school spaces, sports, and community support systems.

Transportation

Explore how public transit accessibility shapes movement across Oakland neighborhoods.

Libraries & Schools

Understand how educational spaces are affected by financial instability.

Housing & Safety

Follow stories about displacement, housing pressure, and neighborhood change.

Community Navigation

Instead of overwhelming residents with statistics and political language, this platform focuses on clarity, accessibility, and lived experiences. The goal is to help Oakland residents navigate systems that directly shape their communities.

Designed To Empower

This project is aimed at Oakland youth, working-class residents, and communities directly impacted by city disinvestment. By making information more visual and approachable, the platform encourages civic awareness and stronger community engagement.

Accessible Information

Financial and policy language translated into visuals and navigation systems that feel understandable.
